How does it work?

The Pomodoro Technique is a way to organize your time and activities, dividing them on small lapses so you can keep your concentrantion and avoid procrastination. The rules are:

  • Decide the task you want to do
  • Start the work temp
  • Do the activity (and only this activity) until the temp ends
  • Take a rest
  • After four pomodoros (four work lapses), take a long rest

In this website, work lapses are 25 minutes long, while rest are 5 minutes and long rest 20 minutes.
